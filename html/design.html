Style Guide

Goal


The goal of the website is to inspire the user by curating a visually captivating and immersive online experience that seamlessly navigation and blends in design, using minimalism, and modern functionality.


Colour


On my website, I use 4 main types of colours. The first two are shades of grey and these are mainly used for the backgrounds. They used the grey-shaded backgrounds as it allowed for more of a visual contrast. As the grey backgrounds provide a neutral base that allows other website elements, such as the text, images, and buttons, to stand out more prominently. The contrast between the shades of grey and the content will enhance readability and make the website more visually appealing. The reason why I used two different shades of grey also is to create a form of separation in the section of the website. The use of grey backgrounds allows the user to focus on the content of the website. By using the subtle grey background, it focuses the users' attention towards the main content of the website. It prevents distractions and ensures that the focus remains on the information and visuals that I want to show. Using grey backgrounds also provided a sense of professionalism and elegance in appearance. The greys often create an elegant aesthetic, lending a professional touch to your website. It helps convey a sense of professionalism, reliability, and seriousness. Using grey background allows for versatility and accessibility. This means that the backgrounds can easily blend with different colour schemes and design elements

Along with the grey backgrounds I used cyan and white for the other element in the website and I did this for:
Readability: White text on a grey background provides a high contrast, making the text easier to read. The use of cyan accents can enhance the visibility and importance of elements on the website. This colour scheme created a modern and clean aesthetic for the website. The combination of shades of grey and cyan with white elements creates a sleek and contemporary look for a website. This is because the colour scheme is associated with a minimalist design approach, which further conveys a sense of professionalism. The use of these colours creates a calm atmosphere on the website. This colour scheme is often used in websites related to wellness, technology, or other industries where a sense of peace is desired.

The use of the colour scheme also represents me as a person in a way. As grey is often associated with neutrality and practicality. I feel this represent me in an aspect as in most situation I try to either avoid conflict and I always try to think about each aspect of a situation to find the best outcome.

Cyan represents me in a way as it is a colour that often represents freshness and innovation. I feel as if this captures a part of me as certain things like food tasting, I always want to try and experience new things.

The layout of a website refers to the arrangement and organization of its various elements and content on the web pages. A well-designed website layout ensures a logical and visually appealing structure that facilitates user navigation and engagement. While website layouts can vary based on design preferences and specific requirements, Here is the base layout for my design:

Header: Located at the top of the page and will always be available to the user, the header will contain the website's logo, navigation menu
Navigation menu: This is a list of clickable links or buttons that allow users to navigate through different sections and pages of the website. It will be in the header.
Content area: This is the main section of the website where the primary content is displayed. This content area is often divided into various sections seen through the grey backgrounds to organize the information of my content.

UX & UI Decisions


UI (User Interface) refers to the visual elements, controls, and layout of a digital product or website that users interact with. It encompasses everything that users see and interact with on a screen, including buttons, menus, forms, icons, and any other visual components. UI design focuses on creating a visually appealing and intuitive interface that enhances user experience.

User Experience (UX) refers to the overall experience that users have when interacting with the website. It encompasses every aspect of the user's interaction, including their perceptions, emotions, and behaviours. UX design aims to create intuitive, meaningful, and enjoyable experiences for the user. Ultimately, UX focuses on delivering a seamless and delightful experience that meets user expectations and drives user engagement.

Overall


Overall, UI (User Interface) and UX (User Experience) play crucial roles in enhancing the overall user satisfaction and effectiveness of a digital product or service. A well-designed UI focuses on creating an intuitive and visually appealing interface, where elements are organized, consistent, and easily understandable. I feel as if I have achieved this as my website facilitates seamless navigation and interaction, allowing users to view and interact with my website efficiently. On the other hand, UX involves the holistic experience a user has while engaging with a website, I feel like I have included factors like usability, accessibility, and emotional engagement to ensure good UX in my website, to allow for functionality, and encounter minimal frustrations. By providing clear information, and minimizing cognitive load, My website's UI and UX contribute to a positive user experiences.
